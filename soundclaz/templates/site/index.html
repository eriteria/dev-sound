Our approach

01

Research

This is where we hold discovery sessions to create user profiles and find out our clients' wants and needs. We also take an in-depth look into history and present trends to uncover a strategy that will grant our clients a competitive edge

02

Prototyping

On the basis of insights gotten from our research phase, we get to work. Concepts in our mood boards and style precepts come to life in a miniature and experimental manner.

03

Presentation

Our clients are the first critics. How does it look to them? Does it pass the message they want? We do this to give our clients the chance to critique what we're about to put out.

04

Implemetation

After our clients have voiced their opinions, it's time to take action. We put up the billboards, shoot the commercials, post the logo, launch the website and much more

05

Monitoring

Our project is now out there, the client is satisfied, but there's always need for icing on the cake. This can be in form of social media management.
