Abstract

Our purpose is to design and manufacture the world's best ice boxes and associated outdoor equipment for informal markets. The toughest and most functional equipment’s that money can buy. Each of our products has been designed for a specific need or market application and has resulted in unique yet functional designs being used all over Africa. Every idea has been conceptualised and developed by investing time in the trade, market and with vendors. Our goal is to keep improving the way things are sold in street markets and to find customers who share our belief in the power of the informal market
